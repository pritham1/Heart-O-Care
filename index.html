❤️ Heart O Care Emergency Assistant

Your quick-response assistant during heart-related emergencies. Input symptoms, get local help, and receive instant treatment advice.

    🩺 Basic First-Aid Tips for Heart Emergencies

    • Stay calm and reassure the patient
    • Loosen tight clothing
    • Make the patient sit and rest
    • Chew and swallow an aspirin (if not allergic)
    • Call emergency services immediately
    • If unconscious and not breathing, begin CPR

    📌 Emergency Checklist

    • Contact nearest hospital
    • Keep emergency contact notified
    • Prepare ID and health documents
    • Monitor breathing and pulse

    🧾 Emergency Treatment Steps

    1. 🫀 Check pulse and breathing
    2. 📞 Call ambulance or emergency helpline
    3. 💊 Give aspirin if the patient is conscious and not allergic
    4. 🛌 Keep the patient calm, warm and rested
    5. ❤️‍🩹 Begin CPR if the patient is unresponsive and not breathing
    6. 🚑 Accompany the patient to hospital with medical records if possible
